Ethics & Governance

Vaidya Vault aligns with the Nagoya Protocol, UNDRIP, and UNESCO safeguarding principles. Every entry carries explicit consent and licensing metadata to protect cultural rights and guide appropriate use.

  • Consent & Access: Open, Restricted, or Community-only visibility.
  • Attribution & Licensing: Source links, DOIs when available, and clear usage notes.
  • Community Oversight: Requests for takedown, redaction, or reclassification are honored.
